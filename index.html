NIU's Cybersecurity and Future Networks Workshop (CFNW)

Digital Forensics and Quantum Safe in 5G-Powered Mission Critical Vehicles

Date: September 11-12, 2025

Location: The Psycology/Computer Science building (PM 55)

Workshop Chair: Dr. Hisham Kholidy, PhD, CEH, IEEE Senior Member,
Cybersecurity Founding Director, Director of the CECFN

Workshop Overview

The workshop will feature practical demonstrations and research presentations in various fields, including live digital forensics, quantum security, critical 5G applications, confidential high-performance computing, and quantum AI for cybersecurity. The workshop will primarily discuss funding opportunities related to the aforementioned research topics.
